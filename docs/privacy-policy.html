Privacy Policy

FocusGuard — Last updated: February 23, 2026

FocusGuard ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

FocusGuard does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information remains exclusively on your device.

2. Permissions and Why We Need Them

  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS — Used to detect which app is currently in the foreground so FocusGuard can enforce your blocking rules. This data is read locally and never leaves your device.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — Used to display the full list of installed apps so you can choose which ones to block. No list is sent externally.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW — Used to display the blocking overlay on top of restricted apps. Required for the core blocking feature to work.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — Used optionally for location-based blocking rules (e.g., block certain apps only when you are at a specific place). Location data is processed on-device and never transmitted.

3. Data Storage

All settings, schedules, and usage statistics created by FocusGuard are stored locally on your device using the Android Room database. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all this data.

4. Third-Party Services

FocusGuard does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDK. No data is shared with any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

FocusGuard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
