FocusGuard is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information with any third party.

1. Who We Are

FocusGuard ("the App") is developed by an independent developer. Contact: contact@focusguard.app

2. Data We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location data
  • The list of apps installed on your device (beyond what is needed locally)
  • Your browsing history or usage patterns sent to any server

All data (XP, level, badges, statistics, blocked apps) is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (SharedPreferences & Room database). Nothing leaves your phone.

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) ‚Äî Required to detect which app is in the foreground so we can block it when needed. This data is read locally and never transmitted.

4. Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics (by Google) to automatically collect crash reports when the app unexpectedly stops. This helps us fix bugs quickly.

Crashlytics may collect:

  • Stack traces and error information (no personal content)
  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • A randomly generated installation ID (not linked to your identity)

This data is governed by Google's Firebase Privacy Policy. You can opt out by uninstalling the app.

5. Third-Party Services

Beyond Firebase Crashlytics, FocusGuard does not integrate any advertising SDK, analytics platform, social login, or data broker.

6. Children's Privacy

FocusGuard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Data Retention

Since all app data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings, or by uninstalling the app. Crashlytics data is retained by Google for 90 days.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (crash reports via Firebase)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@focusguard.app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you via an in-app notice for material changes. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at: contact@focusguard.app
